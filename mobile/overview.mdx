## What the Mobile App Does

The 1nSpecT mobile app is your field companion. Use it to:

- **Capture photos** for each deficiency with automatic GCS upload
- **Generate AI narratives** from field notes with a single tap
- **Run Vision Analysis** — Gemini analyzes your photo and writes the narrative for you
- **Use Co-Inspector** — AI spots secondary deficiencies in your photos and queues them for capture
- **Rate subsections** using I / NI / NP / D directly from the inspection template
- **Review and finalize** findings before publishing

## Getting the App

The 1nSpecT mobile app is available for iOS and Android through the TestFlight beta program during early access.

Contact [support@1nspect.app](mailto:support@1nspect.app) to request a beta invite.

## Quick Start

1. Sign in with your 1nSpecT account credentials
2. Tap an inspection from your queue
3. Navigate to a section and subsection
4. Tap the camera button to capture a photo
5. Add a field note — or tap **Vision** to let AI write it for you
6. Tap **Save** to record the finding and move to the next one
