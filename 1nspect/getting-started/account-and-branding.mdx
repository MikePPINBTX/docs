# Account Setup & Branding

Your first step in 1nSpecT is configuring your company profile and branding. This information appears on every report PDF, email communication, and client-facing portal your clients will see.

---

## Logging In

1. Navigate to [app.1nspect.app](https://app.1nspect.app) (or your custom domain if configured).
2. Enter your email and password, then tap **Sign In**.
3. On first login you will land on the **Dashboard**.

> If you do not have credentials, contact your account administrator. Self-service signup is available at the start of a free trial.

---

## Company Profile

Go to **Settings → Company Profile** to configure your business identity.

### Required Fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company Name** | Appears in report headers, emails, and the client portal |
| **Owner / Inspector Name** | Used in contract templates as `$InspectorName` |
| **License Number** | TREC license or equivalent; appears in contracts and reports as `$InspectorLicense` |
| **Phone** | Displayed in report footers and client emails |
| **Email** | Reply-to address for client communications |
| **Website** | Optional; displayed in report footers |

### Address
Your business address is used in contract documents and certain email templates.

## Branding

Go to **Settings → Branding** to customize the visual identity of all client-facing output.

### Logo
Upload a PNG or SVG logo (recommended size: **400 × 120 px**, transparent background). This logo appears:
- In the header of every PDF report
- In client-facing email communications
- On the client signing portal

### Colors
1nSpecT uses three brand colors across all generated output:

| Setting | Where it appears |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Primary Color** | Report section headers, table borders, primary buttons |
| **Secondary Color** | Accent bars, subsection dividers |
| **Accent Color** | Highlights, badges, AI-generated content markers |

Enter hex values (e.g. `#3232A8`) or use the color picker. Changes apply to all **new** PDFs generated after saving — existing published reports are not retroactively updated.

### Typography
Set the default font family and size for report body text. The report PDF renderer supports:
- System fonts (Arial, Georgia, Times New Roman)
- Google Fonts (loaded at PDF generation time — requires internet on the backend)

### Preview
After saving your branding settings, open any inspection and click **Generate PDF Preview** from the Report Workspace to see your branding applied to a live report.

---

## Report Header & Footer

Go to **Settings → Report Design** to configure what appears in the running header and footer of multi-page reports.

The report header on section pages can include:
- Company logo (left)
- Report title and property address (center)
- Rating legend (I / NI / NP / D) (right)

The footer on all pages includes:
- Page number (auto-calculated)
- Company name and license number
