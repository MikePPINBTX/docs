## The Core Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account & branding">
    Upload your logo, set your brand colors, and configure your company profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your inspection template">
    Use the Schema Builder to create sections, subsections, and data fields — or start with the pre-built TREC Texas Residential template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an order">
    Enter client details, property address, services, and pricing. Contracts are sent automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct the inspection on mobile">
    Capture photos, write captions, and let AI draft your narratives on-site.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish the report">
    Review findings in the Report Workspace, generate the PDF, and deliver to your client in one click.
  </Step>
</Steps>
